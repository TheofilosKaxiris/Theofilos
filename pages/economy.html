Popular Eurostat Economy Dataset API URLs

Below are some of the most useful Eurostat API URLs for economic statistics, each with a short explanation of what data it provides:

  • General government accounts
    Government revenue, expenditure, and balance for EU countries.
    https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/api/dissemination/statistics/1.0/data/gov_10a_main
  • GDP and main components (output, expenditure and income)
    Gross Domestic Product and its main components by country and year.
    https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/api/dissemination/statistics/1.0/data/nama_10_gdp
  • GDP per capita, consumption per capita and price level indices
    GDP per person, actual individual consumption, and price level indices for comparison between countries.
    https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/api/dissemination/statistics/1.0/data/prc_ppp_ind
  • GDP per capita in PPS
    GDP per capita in Purchasing Power Standards (PPS), allowing comparison of living standards.
    https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/api/dissemination/statistics/1.0/data/tec00114
  • Gross value added and income by A*10 industry breakdowns
    Value added and income by main industry groups (A*10 breakdown).
    https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/api/dissemination/statistics/1.0/data/nama_10_a10
  • Employment by sex, age and nationality
    Employment statistics broken down by gender, age group, and nationality.
    https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/api/dissemination/statistics/1.0/data/lfsa_egan
  • Unemployment by sex and age
    Unemployment rates by gender and age group, monthly or quarterly.
    https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/api/dissemination/statistics/1.0/data/une_rt_a
  • Harmonised index of consumer prices (HICP)
    Inflation rates based on the harmonised index of consumer prices.
    https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/api/dissemination/statistics/1.0/data/prc_hicp_aind
  • Government finance statistics (GFS)
    Government deficit, debt, and other finance indicators.
    https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/api/dissemination/statistics/1.0/data/gov_10dd_edpt1
  • Labour cost index
    Trends in labour costs (wages, salaries, and other costs) over time.
    https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/api/dissemination/statistics/1.0/data/lci_r2_a
  • Household saving rate
    Percentage of household income saved, by country and year.
    https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/api/dissemination/statistics/1.0/data/tipsavm
  • Inflation rate (annual change)
    Annual inflation rate for EU countries.
    https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/api/dissemination/statistics/1.0/data/tec00118
  • Disposable income of households
    Net disposable income of households, by country and year.
    https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/api/dissemination/statistics/1.0/data/tespm010
  • Purchasing power standard (PPS) per inhabitant
    Purchasing power per person, adjusted for price differences between countries.
    https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/api/dissemination/statistics/1.0/data/tec00120
  • GDP per capita in euro
    GDP per person, measured in euros.
    https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/api/dissemination/statistics/1.0/data/tec00001
  • GDP per capita in US dollars
    GDP per person, measured in US dollars.
    https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/api/dissemination/statistics/1.0/data/tec00009

For more datasets, visit the Eurostat Database or Eurostat API Guide.
